# World Cup Prediction Strategy Document

## Rules
1. **Establish Baseline Relative Strength and Calibrate Confidence Dynamically**
   Before analyzing specific tactical trends or managerial records, always establish a baseline comparison of both teams using FIFA rankings, Elo ratings, or squad market values. When there is a massive disparity in footballing stature (e.g., a top-tier UEFA giant vs. a lower-ranked CONCACAF nation), ensure both sides are researched, avoid over-indexing on minor tactical vulnerabilities of the favorite, and scale confidence ratings upward (9/10 or 10/10) rather than defaulting to low confidence due to "insufficient data" on the underdog.
   *(Informed by: GER vs CUW)*

## Data Endpoint Evaluation
* **Useful Endpoints:** Basic team profiling metrics (FIFA rankings, Elo ratings, squad market values, and confederation comparisons) are highly valuable for establishing baseline match context and preventing massive miscalculations of team strength.
* **Not Useful Endpoints:** Deep-dive tactical metrics (such as a manager's specific defensive records or recent warm-up fixtures) are of low value if prioritized before establishing the baseline relative strength of the two competing nations.

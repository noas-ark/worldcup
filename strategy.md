# World Cup Prediction Strategy Document

### Active Rules
*   **Expect Tight Margins Against Resilient Underdogs (Informed by HAI vs SCO):** When predicting matches where a favored European team faces a physically resilient and defensively compact underdog (such as competitive CONCACAF sides), expect a tight, low-scoring grind rather than overwhelming offensive dominance. Calibrate confidence levels cautiously to account for low-scoring variance.

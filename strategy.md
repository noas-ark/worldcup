# World Cup Prediction Strategy Document

### Core Prediction Rules
1. **BRA vs MAR: Avoid the "Individual Talent" Trap Against Elite Low Blocks**
Do not back a favorite solely on individual attacking talent (e.g., elite isolated wingers) when facing a highly disciplined, defensively organized low-block team, especially in group stages where the underdog is highly motivated to play for a draw. If the favorite lacks cohesive central playmaking or has key playmaker fitness issues, heavily discount their win probability and seriously consider the draw.

2. **QAT vs SUI: De-bias "On-Paper" Superiority of Possession-Heavy Favorites**
Do not assign high confidence to favorites based purely on squad value, league pedigree, or possession dominance. When a possession-heavy favorite lacks the specific creative capacity to break down a compact defensive structure, their superiority is neutralized. Thoroughly analyze the underdog’s defensive resilience and avoid high-confidence picks on the favorite if a low-block stalemate is likely.
